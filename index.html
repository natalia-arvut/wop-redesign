01 · Cinematic Globe

Cinematic Globe

Mood: a living planet of energy — rich, photoreal, confident.

Idea. A photorealistic Earth with a real offshore platform at its core, glowing logistics arcs and real agent faces pinned to their cities. From the first second it says: oil & gas, worldwide, with a human agent everywhere.

How to develop. The globe becomes the signature across the platform — interactive on the homepage, mini-globes in company profiles and the agent network. Best for wow-factor and investor-facing impact.

Cinematic Editorial

Mood: a global corporation & investment platform — serious, expensive.

Idea. A full-bleed photograph of an offshore platform at golden hour, a bold editorial headline over it, and glass KPI panels. Reads like the annual report of a global energy major — the direction for gravitas and trust with big companies and investors.

03 · Live Data Terminal

Live Data Terminal

Mood: a Bloomberg / Palantir for oil & gas — alive, data-driven, light.

Idea. The homepage is a live control room: a global activity map with routes, pulsing city hubs and floating events (new tender, deal closed, route), plus real-time metric tiles. It sells the platform as infrastructure that never stops working.

How to develop. Dashboard-grade components everywhere — analytics, KPI center, verification and agent activity. Best for the "operating system of the industry" positioning in the tech spec.
